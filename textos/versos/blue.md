Today, I am sad...

I'm feeling blue, I see the sky, see the stars, think over that pretty moonlight, but so empty, ‘Course it doesn't reflect me anyone...

I'm not sad for someone, I'm sad for no one, I'm sad for me, sad by loneliness...

A sadness that travels further than Lucy and her sky of diamonds, a kind of sadness that no piece of cake can heal...

An empty sadness, as empty as me, as light and as darkness, a sadness as empty as loneliness...
